Supported actions and their required parameters:

* `mouse_move`: Move the cursor to specific (x, y) coordinates.
  - Required params: `coordinate`
* `click`: Click the left mouse button at (x, y). (Implicitly moves cursor first).
  - Required params: `coordinate`
* `left_click_drag`: Click and hold left button at current position, drag to new (x, y), then release.
  - Required params: `coordinate`
* `right_click`: Click the right mouse button at (x, y).
  - Required params: `coordinate`
* `middle_click`: Click the middle mouse button at (x, y).
  - Required params: `coordinate`
* `double_click`: Double-click the left mouse button at (x, y).
  - Required params: `coordinate`
* `scroll`: Scroll the mouse wheel. Positive `pixels` scrolls down/right, negative scrolls up/left.
  - Required params: `pixels` (optionally `coordinate` to scroll at a specific location)
* `type`: Type a string of text. (Ensure text field is focused first).
  - Required params: `text`
* `key`: Press specific keyboard keys (e.g., "Return", "Control+c").
  - Required params: `keys`
* `wait`: Wait for the screen to update or load.
  - Required params: `time`
* `terminate`: Complete the task explicitly.
  - Required params: `status`
